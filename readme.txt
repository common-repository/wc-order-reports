== Description ==
###Order Reports for WooCommerce
**Quickly download and show WooCommerce  sale reporting**

The Order Reports for WooCommerce plugin is easy to integrate with your WooCommerce store. the order reports for the WooCommerce plugin are useful to understanding your business order selling analysis.

The plugin report helps them view data related to their total sale, net sale, total order, shipping, taxes, etc to help to grow their business. also gives the order status-wise sales information.

For order sales analysis, Business users are able to download and view order sales reports including the order items information, shipping and TAX.

==REPORTING FEATURES==

*   Order summary reports like total sale, net sale, total order, average order value, refund order, refund amount, discount amount, shipping, shipping TAX, TAX.
*   Order status-wise sales performance summary.
*   Download and show orders wise product item details, shipping rate, discount, TAX, total in excel file. 
*   Faster performance to display and download sale orders report
*   User has the option to select the date range for the get the orders report detail
*   Sale analytics with multiple sale metrics
*	Order sales report chart represents the various analysis data like Total Sales, Net Sales, Total Orders, Average order value, Refund orders, Refund amount, Discount amount, Total TAX, Order TAX, Shipping TAX, Total shipping, Total users, Unique Users, Orders Status.

= What does the plugin do? =
The plugin is useful to show and download Woocommerce Orders detail information and also the Woocommerce product order analytics reports details.
